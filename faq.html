Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about our data, compliance, and process.

Do you serve consumers?

No. We only serve qualified business clients with a documented permissible purpose under GLBA/DPPA and applicable law.

Where is your data from?

We aggregate from multiple reputable sources and apply human QA. We do not resell raw databases; we deliver resolved and scored outputs.

What’s your typical turnaround?

24–48 hours for batches; same‑day available for priority lists.

Is there an API?

Yes — a lightweight queue API for ongoing drops. Most clients start with secure CSV/SFTP.

How do you handle litigant or DNC risk?

We can flag potential litigants and scrub against known DNC lists upon request. Final use is governed by client policy and applicable law.

How do we start?

Send a note from the Contact page. We’ll confirm scope, sign an agreement, and provide a secure upload link.
